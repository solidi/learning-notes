## App Utils

Utils that cover handling debugging and information on application packaging on macOS.

1. ``codesign  --display --verbose --entitlements - ~/Downloads/SomeApp.app``
1. ``plutil Somefile.plist``
1. ``tccutil reset AddressBook``
